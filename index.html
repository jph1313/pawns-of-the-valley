Pawns of the Valley

March your pawns across the valley and into the enemy camp.

Goal

Get pawns into either of your opponent's two home rows (the glowing camp). Every pawn there scores — stacks count too. Fill their back row (all 4 squares) for an instant win; otherwise the most pawns delivered wins once no moves remain — ties go to most captures.

Setup

Each game opens with a secret deployment. Place your 8 pawns anywhere in your two home rows: click a glowing square to drop one and stack freely; right-click or Undo to remove; Standard fills the classic line. Both sides commit privately, then reveal.

Moving

Pawns move like chess pawns — forward one square, or two from their second row. They walk down into the valley freely.

Climbing & the valley

Climb straight forward onto a pawn or stack of any height, but only one level higher than you stand. You can't scale the valley wall directly — to reach the far side, climb onto a pawn at the valley's edge, then step across. Only the top pawn of a stack can move; the one beneath is stuck until it leaves.

Capturing

Capture diagonally forward onto an enemy on your level or anywhere below (downhill) — never uphill. The captured pawn is removed.

Controls

Click a pawn, then a highlight — green: move / climbred: capture (or click the red-ringed enemy directly). Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, and switch 3D / Top / Side views below. New game, Undo, and mute sit in the panel; your game auto-saves.
